About

24-year old Suyash, made his FC debut in 2018 for Goa. Suyash was bought by the RCB for INR 30 lakhs at the IPL 2022 Mega Auction. As of today, Suyash has played a total of 22 T20 games (Syed Mushtaq Ali) with 443 runs to his name and a strike rate of 148 plus. Suyash is all set to Play Bold.
